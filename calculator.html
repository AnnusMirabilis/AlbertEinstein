Mass-Energy Equivalence

This formula, developed by Albert Einstein, calculates the energy (E) contained in a given amount of mass (m). The speed of light squared (c2) is the conversion factor.

Photoelectric Effect

This effect describes the emission of electrons from a metal when light shines on it. It calculates the kinetic energy (KE) of the emitted electron.
